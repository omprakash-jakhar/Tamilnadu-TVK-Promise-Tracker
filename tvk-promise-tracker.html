How to read this tracker

A Note on Good Faith

This tracker was created with genuine good faith — we believe in holding elected governments accountable to the promises they made publicly, and we want this TVK government to succeed in fulfilling them for the people of Tamil Nadu.

We are ordinary citizens, not journalists. If you know something has been done, started, or abandoned — please tell us.

Methodology & Standards

Source of promises: All promises are drawn directly from TVK's official manifesto released on 29 March 2026 and verified against news reports from the election campaign.
Source hierarchy: (1) Official government gazette or GO. (2) Cabinet statement or press release. (3) PTI/ANI wire reports in national publications. Social media posts do not qualify.
"In Progress" means a formal order, committee, or allocation has been initiated — with source link. Intent alone does not qualify.
"Evaded" means government action has made fulfilment structurally impossible. Pending means it could still happen.
Term tracked: Oath — 10 May 2026. All promises tracked over the full 5-year term ending May 2031.
